## A.S. Neill (Summerhill) — interviews, biography, official school media

**Yes, there is footage — but it’s thin compared to Gatto/Holt.** Neill died in **1973**, so almost everything is **1960s–early-1970s television** (or later documentaries that **cut in** that archival material). There is **no** large modern talking-head archive of Neill himself.

### Neill on camera (speech / interview — best primary sources on YouTube)

- [A. S. Neill — founder of Summerhill (1964 TV: *Here and Now*)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4-C2i9Iq9vY) (~13 min — freedom, play, self-government, anti-compulsion)
- [Summerhill founder A.S. Neill shares his wisdom — long interview](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SJP0GTEtj9M) (~31 min — deep-dive Q&A; uploaded by Education Options TV)

### Documentary / trailer (mixed archival Neill + later context)

- [*Imagine a School… Summerhill* — preview](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GdwjvxcJHTA) (William Tyler Smith; Neill as historical figure + school story)
